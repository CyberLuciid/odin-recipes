Jalapeno Poppers

Description

These breaded jalapeno poppers are the perfect combination of crispy and spicy. Whether you want them as a snack, an appetizer, or you want fill your stomach to the brim with them, they're perfect for any occasion!

Ingredients


  • 10 fresh jalapenos
  • 1 1/4 Cups (150g) your choice of breadcrumbs
  • 2 Teaspoons garlic powder, divided
  • 1 1/2 Teaspoons salt, divided
  • 1 Teaspoon dried parsley
  • 1/2 Cup (120g) cream cheese
  • 1/2 to 2/3 Cup finely grated cheddar or Monterrey Jack cheese
  • Handful of finely sliced chives/green onions
  • 1 Cup (130g) plain flour/ all-purpose flour
  • Approx. 7oz of cold water (200ml)
  • Oil for deep frying
